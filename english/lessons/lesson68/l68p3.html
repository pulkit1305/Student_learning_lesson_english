Learning objectives

Learning objectives

In this lesson, students will learn to:

  • explore the impact of pathetic fallacy
  • explore ways in which writers create settings and their impact.

Key definitions

Subject vocabulary

  • figurative language: a language device creating a comparison to convey a vivid image, most commonly through simile, metaphor or personification
  • juxtaposition: putting two very different things close together to encourage comparison between them
  • pathetic fallacy: a device in which the setting reflects or foreshadows the action in a scene, for example a storm suggesting imminent danger or emotional turmoil
